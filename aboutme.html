About Me

A Little Bit About Me

I graduated from Rhode Island College with a Bachelor of Arts and Science Degree in Graphic Design in May of 2020. I like to think outside the box in any situation especially with art. I try and create designs that have a new and unique feel. When I was a little kid, I did not realize it then, but I was already designing on paper. I used to design newspapers for fun. I would write the articles and draw the pictures while also doing the layouts. When I got a little older, I got into the game The Sims and I began designing websites and creating clothes that my characters could wear. I really appreciate a good logo to the point where I find myself obsessively staring at billboards, trucks, and anything where a logo would be.
